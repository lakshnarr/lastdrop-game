Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Introduction

Last Drop ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application and website collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Camera Permission

Our app requests camera access for the following purpose:

  • QR Code Scanning: To scan QR codes for joining live games as a spectator

We do NOT:

  • Record or store photos/videos
  • Access your photo gallery
  • Share camera data with third parties
  • Use camera for any purpose other than QR code scanning

Bluetooth Permission

Our app uses Bluetooth to connect to:

  • GoDice electronic dice
  • ESP32 LED game board

Bluetooth data is used only for game functionality and is not stored or shared.

Game Data

We collect minimal game-related information:

  • Player names (you provide)
  • Game scores and statistics
  • Roll history for replay features

How We Use Your Information

  • Operate the game and its features
  • Display live games to spectators
  • Generate leaderboards and analytics
  • Provide game replay functionality
  • Improve user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and on our secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Retention: Game data is retained for analytics and replay purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

Children's Privacy

Last Drop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app does not require account creation or personal information to play.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt out of data collection (by not using optional features)
  • Deny camera/Bluetooth permissions (app functionality will be limited)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
